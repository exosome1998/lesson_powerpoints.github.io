Level 7: Calculating Wave Speed /8

Formula: v = f × λ

1. Walkie-Talkie: f = 50,000, λ = 6,000
v = 50,000 × 6,000 = 300,000,000 m/s
2. Satellite: f = 3,000,000, λ = 100
v = 3,000,000 × 100 = 300,000,000 m/s

3. Pattern: For all EM waves, speed (v) is 300,000,000 m/s.

4. Light goes around the Earth 7.5 times in 1 second.

5. Which is faster: Sound or Wi-Fi? Wi-Fi.

6. Sound (v=340, f=170): λ = 340 ÷ 170 = 2 m.

7. Can humans hear EM waves directly? ( No )

8. We use speakers to turn them into sound waves.

Level 8: Wavelength Bootcamp /8

Formula: λ = v ÷ f

1. FM Radio: v = 300,000,000, f = 100,000,000
λ = 300,000,000 ÷ 100,000,000 = 3 m
2. AM Radio: v = 300,000,000, f = 1,000,000
λ = 300,000,000 ÷ 1,000,000 = 300 m

3. Formula for f: f = v ÷ λ

4. v = 300,000,000. λ = 150m. f = 2,000,000 Hz.

5. v = 300,000,000. λ = 5m. f = 60,000,000 Hz.

6. Higher frequency numbers mean more energy.

7. 1,000,000 Hz is 1 MegaHertz (MHz).

8. Boss: v = 3E8, f = 3E8. λ = 1 m.
