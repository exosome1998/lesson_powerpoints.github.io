Level 9: Calculating Wavelength /8

Formula: λ = v ÷ f

1. v = 300 m/s, f = 100 Hz
λ = 300 ÷ 100 = 3 m
2. v = 1500 m/s, f = 500 Hz
λ = 1500 ÷ 500 = 3 m
3. v = 340 m/s, f = 170 Hz
λ = 340 ÷ 170 = 2 m

4. To divide easily, you can cancel out the zeros.

5. A high frequency wave has a ( Shorter / Longer ) wavelength.

6. Wavelength of a 10Hz wave at 20m/s = 2 m.

7. λ = 100 ÷ 20 = 5 m.

8. Wavelength is distance per wave.
