SECTION 4: TRIANGLE SCAFFOLDING
To find v: f × λ
v = Velocity / Speed (m/s)
f = Frequency (Hz)
λ = Wavelength (m)
To find f: v ÷ λ
To find λ: v ÷ f

SECTION 6: WORD STORIES (High-Volume Word Problems)
1. The Ultrasound: A medical scanner uses a wave with frequency 2,000,000 Hz. The speed in the body is 1540 m/s.
Wavelength? (λ = v ÷ f)
Ans: 0.00077 m
2. The Seismic Wave: An earthquake wave travels at 5000 m/s. If its wavelength is 2500 m...
Frequency? (f = v ÷ λ)
Ans: 2 Hz
3. The Ripples: A stone dropped in a pond creates 24 semi-circles in 4 seconds. The wavelength is 0.5 m.
First find f, then v.
Speed: 1.5 m/s
4. The Deep Bass: A speaker emits a low note with wavelength 10m. Sound speed is 340 m/s.
Calculate f.
Ans: 34 Hz
5. The Piano Wire: A middle C note vibrates at 262 Hz. If the speed of sound is 344 m/s...
Calculate λ.
Ans: 1.31 m
6. The Radio Tower: A station broadcasts at 90,000,000 Hz. The speed is 300,000,000 m/s.
Calculate λ.
Ans: 3.33 m
7. The Bat: A bat emits a 40,000 Hz chirp. The wavelength is 0.0085 m.
Find the speed of sound.
Ans: 340 m/s
8. The Tsunami: A massive wave has a wavelength of 200,000 m and f = 0.001 Hz.
How fast is it moving?
Ans: 200 m/s
